Let $A_{1}, A_{2}, \ldots$ be [[Independent and Identically Distributed (IID)|IID]] random variables and $\mathbf{E}[A_{i}] = \mu$ is finite, then
$$\frac{1}{m} \sum\limits_{i = 1}^{m} A_{i} \rightarrow_{p} \mu$$
